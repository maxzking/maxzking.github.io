Gravitational Synthesizer

Summary

This synthesizer is governed by Newton's Laws of Universal Gravitation. In this patch, there is a planet and a stationary sun. You control the mass of the sun as well as the planet's initial velocity and distance from the sun. The sound evolves with the position and velocity of the planet. See if you can keep the planet in a stable orbit!

Background

The first version of this patch was created for Music 158A "Music and Computing" during my undergraduate studies at UC Berkeley. At the time, I was most interested in modeling some of the concepts I had learned from physics with sound. Our assignment was to create a patch capable of synthesizing at least 4 different sounds.

The motion of the planet is described by Newton's Law of Universal Gravitation. The patch calculates the force of attraction between the planet and the sun in real-time.

Features

  • Stereo Imaging
  • 5 Synthesizer Presets
  • Graphical Display
