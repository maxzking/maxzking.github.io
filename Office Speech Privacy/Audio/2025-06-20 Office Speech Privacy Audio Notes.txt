About the audio in this Folder:

"select_sound" and "select_sound_plus_1" are audio files that play each time a button is pressed.

The rest of the audio follows this name structure: location-volume-partition-masking
The values for location are: receive1, receive2, source-room
The values for volume are: N (normal speech), R (raised speech)
The values for partition (wall and door types) are: Partial Height, Single Layer Full Height, Double Layer One Side, Double Layer Both Sides, Sliding, Swing (Swing door, Bursh seals, no drop bottom), swing+ (swing door, bulb seals, drop bottom)
The values for masking are: ON, OFF
